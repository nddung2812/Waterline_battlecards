# Waterline Competitor Battlecards

Interactive competitive battlecard for Waterline Projects covering 11 competitors in the QLD/NSW resources engineering market, grouped into three tiers (regional rivals, specialists & advisories, global giants).

Open `index.html` in a browser, or view the live page (GitHub Pages) once enabled.

- Threat read, comparison matrix, and per-competitor battlecards (pitch, strengths, vulnerabilities, how Waterline wins, objection handling).
- Research date: June 2026. Refresh quarterly.

> Size, revenue and follower figures are third-party aggregator estimates — verify before customer-facing use.
